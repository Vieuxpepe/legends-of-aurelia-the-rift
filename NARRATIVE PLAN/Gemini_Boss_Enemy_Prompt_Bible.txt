================================================================================
LEGENDS OF AURELIA — GEMINI PROMPT BIBLE: NAMED BOSSES ONLY
================================================================================
**Art Director — use one file:** `NARRATIVE PLAN/Art_Director_Boss_Portrait_And_Battle_Sprite_Sourcebook.md`
has global direction + every boss portrait/battle prompt in one place. This file
remains a plain-text duplicate for tooling / search; prefer the sourcebook for humans.

Purpose: Reusable text prompts for Google Gemini (image or multimodal concepting)
for **named boss characters** in Legends of Aurelia. Excludes generic / roster
enemy archetypes (Sun Archer, League Guard, etc.).

How to use:
- **Check FINISHED BOSS ART first** — five bosses already have portrait + battle
  sprite PNGs in the repo; skip fresh generation unless you are matching style or
  replacing files.
- **Dialogue / UI / dossier art** → paste **MASTER PORTRAIT BLOCK**, then the
  boss’s **PORTRAIT PROMPT** (and optional AVOID / NOTES).
- **Tactical battle sprite, unit strip, combat roster** → paste **MASTER BATTLE
  SPRITE BLOCK**, then the boss’s **BATTLE SPRITE PROMPT** (and optional AVOID).
- Keep prompts in English unless you are localizing marketing copy separately.
- For recurring bosses (same name, harder encounter), add one line to either prompt:
  “More battle-worn / higher ritual corruption than earlier appearance.”

Source synthesis: NARRATIVE PLAN/Boss_Artstyle_Production_Sheet.txt,
Scripts/BossUnitDataGenerator.gd (classes & signature weapons),
Scripts/Narrative/BossPersonalDialogueDB.gd (voice and threat profile).

================================================================================
MASTER PORTRAIT BLOCK (dialogue / character sheet / bust art)
================================================================================
Dark fantasy **character portrait**. Chest-up or waist-up, centered composition;
face clearly readable at small UI scale (~35–45% of frame height). Slight three-
quarter turn OK. High-contrast lighting on face and eyes, grounded materials
(metal, cloth, leather, ritual details). Painterly or high-quality illustration;
NOT chibi, NOT gacha, NOT Fortnite, NOT photorealistic stock photo. No modern
clothing, logos, or sci-fi tech. Serious adult tone. Hands only if simple
(book edge, staff rest); avoid elaborate gestures that muddy a small crop.
Clean background or soft abstract mood (smoke, sigils, bokeh) — not busy scenery.
No text or watermarks.

================================================================================
MASTER BATTLE SPRITE BLOCK (full-body tactical / combat roster)
================================================================================
Dark fantasy **full-body tactical battle illustration**. Entire figure from head
to toe; strong readable silhouette; weapon and armor role obvious at a glance.
Dynamic combat-ready or commanding stance; feet planted; weight believable.
Simple ground plane or subtle shadow; optional faint hex-grid or stone floor
suggestion — **no UI frames, no stat text**. Slight isometric-friendly or three-
quarter front view (JRPG battle sprite convention OK). Materials grounded; palette
controlled. NOT chibi, NOT gacha, NOT Fortnite. No modern items. No text or
watermarks.

================================================================================
WORLD TONE (optional short prefix)
================================================================================
Late-medieval / early-Renaissance fantasy realm at war. Factions include: a
militant church-state (Valeron), a predatory merchant league, occult rift cultists
(Obsidian Circle), frontier loggers and wardens, and drowning cosmic forces (Dark Tide).

01 — LADY VESPERA
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SUMMARY: Obsidian Circle mastermind; cold ceremonial authority; rift/ash magic;
         priestly stillness that can snap into violence. Escalates: apparition =
         more spectral; late game = more corporeal void-touched certainty.

FACTION / THREAT: Obsidian Circle | void ritualist | catalyst antagonist

SIGNATURE GEAR (game): Rift Rite Tome; fire-sage / ritual caster read

PORTRAIT PROMPT:
[MASTER PORTRAIT BLOCK] Lady Vespera, formidable antagonist priestess of a rift
cult. High ceremonial collar, asymmetrical occult detail, void-weave fabrics,
fractured ritual metal at shoulders or throat. Palette: black-violet, controlled
blood-red accents, cold lunar rim light on face. Expression: intelligent, patient
cruelty; eyes that judge. Faint ash and rift bloom at frame edges only.

BATTLE SPRITE PROMPT:
[MASTER BATTLE SPRITE BLOCK] Lady Vespera full body, ritual caster stance: one
hand on open Rift Rite Tome, other hand raised with gathering void flame. Long
ceremonial layers with asymmetrical occult trim; hem and sleeves readable in
silhouette. Black-violet and ember accent; ash particles minimal. Ground shadow
only; commanding, still-before-storm posture.

AVOID: generic “sexy sorceress”, pin-up, cartoon evil queen, Sauron pastiche.

NOTES: Portrait + sprite **softer and more translucent** for apparition;
       **sharper armor and harder light** for late-game duels.

# Generic Neutral Faction Roster Reference

This is the first-wave **generic neutral / allied green-unit pack** for Aurelia.

Design rules used:
- no abilities
- no death quotes
- no portraits / sprites
- fixed stats
- built for named-neutral support maps and coalition maps

## Why these packs exist

The neutral ally design already supports four battlefield behaviors: green defenders, conditional reinforcements, temporary controllable allies, and off-map strategic allies. Local soldiers, wardens, reformist clergy, escort guards, and faction security are explicitly called out as the right kind of supporting units for this system. The setting and battle plans also support faction security and guarded zones on Maps 7, 8, 9A, 11, and 17.

# Legends of Aurelia: The Rift  
## Updated Battle Map Plan (All Levels, Current Canon)

This is the updated campaign battle-map plan reflecting:
- the current 25-unit roster,
- the exploration-mode chapters,
- fame checks,
- the Tower of Trials structure,
- and the addition of **Haldor the traveling blacksmith**, first encountered in **Map 3**.

This version supersedes earlier battle-plan drafts.

---

# Level 1 — Prologue: The Razed Village

## Narrative Context
Lady Vespera’s cult razes Oakhaven in a soul-harvesting assault. The protagonist is overwhelmed, villagers die around them, and the sky begins to fracture. This battle teaches panic, urgency, and helplessness.

## Map Layout
A burning village map:
- narrow alleys,
- flaming houses,
- central square,
- rubble choke points,
- northern escape edge,
- civilians trapped in homes.

## Battle Gimmick
**Survive and Rescue**
- Survive 6 turns.
- Escort at least 3 civilians to the northern edge.
- Interact with doors to release hidden survivors.
- Fire spreads each turn.

## Tower of Trials Unlock
**Floor 1: Echoing Battlefield**

## Recruitments / Character Intros
- **Kaelen** joins during the map as the veteran rescuer.
- If enough civilians are saved, this sets up **Mira Ashdown** for recruitment next chapter.

---

# Level 2 — Flight through Emberwood

## Narrative Context
Kaelen leads the protagonist and surviving refugees through the Emberwood while cultists pursue them. This is a retreat chapter, not a victory chapter.

## Map Layout
A tight forest escape route:
- dense trees,
- burning southern edge,
- mud patches,
- split path,
- corrupted clearing,
- fallen-log chokepoint,
- northern exit.

## Battle Gimmick
**Escape Under Pressure**
- Reach the northern exit.
- Burning ground spreads from behind.
- Mud lowers movement.
- Rift-scar tiles empower magic but injure the unit standing on them.
- Corrupted beasts appear at the mid-map clearing.

## Tower of Trials Unlock
**Floor 2: Forest of Whispers**

## Recruitments / Character Intros
### Branik
- Enemy block on turn 2.
- Recruited via Talk by choosing empathy and purpose.

### Mira Ashdown
- If at least 3 civilians were saved in Map 1, Mira appears near the refugee wagon.
- She is not a battlefield enemy; she becomes an automatic post-map recruit after proving herself by covering the retreat with a hunting bow.

### Pell Rowan
- Found hiding among the refugee survivors.
- Joins automatically after the chapter as a weak but eager trainee unit.

# Level 3 — Shattered Sanctum

## Exploration Phase
### Setting
The ruined sanctuary outskirts:
- collapsed cloisters,
- looted side rooms,
- broken shrines,
- scattered crates,
- hidden infirmary corner,
- hungry scavengers and displaced peasants.

### Key Interactions
- Speak to survivors sheltering near the temple.
- Find early consumables and lore scraps.
- Optional ruined infirmary scene that sets up **Tamsin Reed**.
- Meet **Haldor the traveling blacksmith** for the first time.

### Fame Consequence
- **Low Fame:** Beggars harass the protagonist and lower starting HP.
- **Neutral Fame:** Standard.
- **High Fame:** Children reveal a back way into the sanctum.

## Special Character Event — Haldor the Traveling Blacksmith
Haldor is first met in this chapter, scavenging the sanctum’s abandoned storage before enemy thieves steal everything.

### Haldor Scene Purpose
- Introduces the blacksmith as a recurring camp / hub figure.
- Establishes his personality: stoic, proud, deeply attached to tools and craft.
- Gives the player an early non-combat side objective.

### Additional Side Objective
**“Protect the Sanctum Stores”**
- Enemy thieves spawn near supply rooms and try to flee with loot.
- If too many escape, later forge inventory is worse.
- If the player protects the supplies, Haldor will later offer better stock and forging options.

## Narrative Context
The party shelters in the sanctum. Ephrem and templars move to purge it. Liora is trapped inside.

## Map Layout
Temple defense map:
- double halls,
- pillars,
- catwalks,
- sealed side-room,
- side storage chambers,
- shrine altars.

## Battle Gimmick
**Collapse and Hold**
- Collapse pillars to block routes.
- Activate altars to purify tiles.
- Stop thief units from escaping off the edge with sanctum loot.
- Rescue Liora before capture.

## Tower of Trials Unlock
**Floor 3: Hall of Statues**

## Recruitments / Character Intros
### Liora
- Recruited by rescuing her from the sealed room with empathetic dialogue.

### Tamsin Reed
- If Liora is rescued and the infirmary was explored, Tamsin joins after the map as a frightened but talented healer apprentice.

### Haldor
- Not a combat unit.
- Introduced here as recurring blacksmith / crafting NPC.
- Future forge quality can be influenced by whether his scavenged materials were protected.

- rooftop ladders,
- sewer grates,
- back-stall markets,
- loose planks over canals,

### Key Interactions
- Black-market stall browsing.
- Rumors on League corruption.
- Secret sewer shortcut.

### Fame Consequence
- **Low Fame:** Harassment and worse start state.

Urban grid:
- rooftops,
- markets,
- civilian lanes,
- sewers,
- watch routes,
- warehouse access points.

## Battle Gimmick
**Civilian Panic**
- Combat near civilians causes chaos.
- Shout clears panicked crowds.
- Rooftops are key tactical space.

## Tower of Trials Unlock
**Floor 4: Market Gauntlet**

## Recruitments / Character Intros
### Rufus
- Recruited via Talk with Nyx or Darian.

### Nyx
- This chapter functions as Nyx’s true party integration chapter if not already traveling with the group.

### Hest “Sparks”
- Recruited through exploration before the battle.

# Haldor Integration Summary

**Haldor the Traveling Blacksmith**
- First met in **Map 3** during the Shattered Sanctum exploration phase.
- He is scavenging abandoned sanctum stores before thieves steal everything.
- Protecting the sanctum supplies improves future forge inventory and his trust.
- After Greyspire is secured, Haldor becomes the army’s recurring blacksmith / crafting anchor at the hub.

He is not currently planned as a playable unit, but he is now fully integrated into the campaign flow as a recurring support NPC with economic and progression value.
